OUR STORY

A Legacy of Excellence, Craftsmanship, and Innovation

The Baretta Heritage

Founded in 1999, Baretta emerged from a vision to redefine luxury fashion. What began as a small atelier in Milan has evolved into a global symbol of sophistication and style.

Every piece we create tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship, sourced from the finest materials across the world. Our artisans blend traditional techniques with contemporary innovation.

Our Journey

1999

The Beginning

Baretta was founded in Milan by renowned designer Alessandro Baretta, opening our first boutique atelier.

2005

Global Expansion

Expanded internationally with flagship stores in Paris, London, and New York, establishing our global presence.

2012

Heritage Collection

Launched our Heritage Collection, celebrating timeless designs with modern craftsmanship and sustainable practices.

2018

Bespoke Service

Introduced our bespoke service, offering personalized luxury experiences to discerning clientele worldwide.

2024

25th Anniversary

Celebrating 25 years of excellence with exclusive collections and continued commitment to unparalleled quality.
